**Farhot - MARS (2025)**

A catchy homage to East African music with its intricate samples. The track is built around the song [_Tilman Baa Lagu Socdaa fra 1987 av den somalske supergruppa 4Mars_](https://rhythmpassport.com/daily-discovery-farhot-mars/). This is fusion to the core, with a feel of a homemade, analog soundscape where Farhot honors Mogadishu's golden days with his own Berlin-inspired underground culture.
