Aloha! I’m an [INFJ](https://www.16personalities.com/infj-personality) from Colombia 🇨🇴, currently rooted in  Norway 🇳🇴. 

I like to explore the balance between modern life and our ancient connection to the earth. I am passionate about [rewilding strategies](https://www.arthurhaines.com/blog/2014/6/13/rewilding-fundamentals), evolutionary astrology, [agorism](https://simple.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agorism) and decentralized privacy tools that support personal freedom. 

I look for complementary patterns in nature and in human relationships. Yin and yang, masculine and feminine, structure and flow.

Music is one of my ways of tuning into the world and other humans. I share songs through [Deichman music blog](https://musikk.deichman.no/) (in Norwegian), and my playlists are available [here](https://open.spotify.com/user/jujudario).

I have been working as a [kambo practitioner](https://iakp.org/user/637/) 🐸 since 2017, and at [Deichman](https://deichman.no/ansatte/149d59f3-c3ef-431f-9dff-da53e9d2b948) 📚 since 2022. But my most important job is being a dad. Nowadays she is drawing [cartoons](https://juju.casa/tegneserier-luna).

This digital webgarden serves as my timeline & online identity inspired by some [Indieweb](https://indieweb.org/) principles. Using social media silos feels like a trap, and true freedom is in having your own domain where no-one can sensor you (yes, I'm highly idealistic; even at the cost of having to learn how to make a website like this). I write here to understand myself and to record my experiments in living, not to convince anyone.
