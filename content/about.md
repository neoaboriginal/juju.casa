Welcome to Casa de JuJu 👋🏼
{{< /lead >}}

I’m an [INFJ](https://www.16personalities.com/infj-personality) from Colombia 🇨🇴, currently rooted in  Norway 🇳🇴. 

I explore the balance between modern life and our ancient connection to the earth - [Rewilding strategies](https://www.arthurhaines.com/blog/2014/6/13/rewilding-fundamentals), small‑scale sovereignty, voluntary exchange, and decentralized tools that support personal freedom. 

In my worldview there is a sense that complementary forces are always in motion — yin and yang, masculine and feminine, structure and flow. I look for these patterns in nature and in human relationships.

Music is one of my ways of tuning into the world. My playlists range from anything like electronic dreamscapes and atmospheric layers to organic and warm rhythms. I share songs through Deichman music blog.

I have been working as a [kambo practitioner](https://iakp.org/user/637/) 🐸 since 2017, and at [Deichman](https://deichman.no/ansatte/149d59f3-c3ef-431f-9dff-da53e9d2b948) 📚 since 2022.

This digital webgarden serves as my timeline & online identity inspired by some [Indieweb](https://indieweb.org/) principles. This site is a record of my experiments in living. I write here to understand myself, not to convince anyone.
