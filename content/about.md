Aloha! I’m an [INFJ](https://www.16personalities.com/infj-personality) from Colombia 🇨🇴, currently rooted in  Norway 🇳🇴. 

I like to explore the balance between modern life and our ancient connection to the earth. I look for complementary patterns in nature and in human relationships. Yin and yang, masculine and feminine, structure and flow.

I am passionate about [rewilding strategies](https://www.arthurhaines.com/blog/2014/6/13/rewilding-fundamentals), [agorism](https://simple.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agorism), sociology, genital sun exposure, evolutionary astrology and decentralized privacy tools that support personal freedom. 

Music is one of my ways of tuning into the world and other humans. I share songs through [Deichman music blog](https://musikk.deichman.no/) (in Norwegian), and my playlists are available on [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/user/jujudario). 

I have been working as a [kambo practitioner](https://iakp.org/user/637/) 🐸 since 2017, and at [Deichman](https://deichman.no/ansatte/149d59f3-c3ef-431f-9dff-da53e9d2b948) 📚 since 2022. But my most important job is being a dad. Nowadays she is drawing [cartoons](https://juju.casa/tegneserier-luna).

Casa de JuJu is a digital webgarden that serves as my timeline & online identity inspired by some [Indieweb](https://indieweb.org/) principles. True digital freedom is in having your own domain where no-one can sensor you (yes, I'm highly idealistic; even at the cost of having to learn how to make a website like this). I post here to leave a modern legacy, to understand myself and to record my experiments of living in the matrix.
